Rayquaza

  • Rayquaza is a generation three pokemon that comes from Hoenn region.
  • He is the main legendary of Pokémon: Emerald, the third game in the Hoenn region.
  • His pokedex number is #384.
  • In the pokedex, he is between Groundon, at #383 and Jirachi at #385.
